About Me

I have a PhD in Telecommunication Engineering and Computer Sceince worked at the biometrics and data pattern analytics lab (BidaLab), Universidad Autónoma de Madrid, Spain. I work as a researcher at the Intelligent Multimodal Vision Analysis (IMVA group), Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF) on 2D/3D Head Pose Estimation.

RESEARCH INTERESTS

I am interested in computer vision using deep learning and machine learning. In particular, I am obsessed with the security and privacy aspects of artificial intelligence.

BACKGROUND

I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science from the Islamic Azad University, in Iran, in 2011, and my M.S. in Informtion Security and Assurance in 2016. In Februray 2021, I started my PhD studies on biometrics using computer vision, and deep learning with a prestigious Marie Skłodowska-Curie scholarship in the EU ITN research program PriMa and I finished my PhD in June 2024. Throughout my doctoral research, I specialized in machine learning where I investigated state-of-the-art computer vision problems including adversarial examples, image morphing, and federated learning on deep neural networks and I published my findings in prestigious venues.
